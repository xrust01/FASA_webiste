Who We Are
 

Financial and Statistical Advisory is an innovative consulting company full of passionate professionals with both excellent education in statistics, econometrics, optimization and probability and broad experience in practical applications of methods and techniques belonging to these areas. Our main focus lies in delivering state-of-the-art solutions to clients from different business fields. We assist our customers in identifying spots where data-driven methods can provide significant improvement in their business processes, we create tailored, sophisticated statistical models and make it possible for them to achieve their most important goals. Top quality academic education, know-how and hands-on experience are the key factors which enable us to develop and implement flexible and robust models with great predictive accuracy which meet all demands and requirements of our clients.

What We Offer

Risk Management Advisory

We help our clients to identify and control risks associated with their business, as that is crucial for achieving long-term growth. We focus especially on risks related to financial positions, mostly from credit risk (scoring models, PD and LGD modelling) or market risk area (yield curve scenarios, stress testing, forecasts of clients’ behaviour). Still, our abilities are of much broader range. We develop statistical models tailored to our customer's needs, which based on market data and company's position can efficiently predict future outcomes and hence provide valuable information to the decision maker. Thanks to our deep understanding of statistical methods and excellent educational background, our models can provide the cutting edge which is often the key factor behind avoiding unnecessary losses.

Statistical Consulting

Statistics is a powerful tool in objectively investigating both research and business questions. We can provide you with a data-driven answer built on strong foundations of a statistical model, which will represent a good advisor when both analysing results of a project or deciding about future investment. We can apply data-science techniques and assist in processing already obtained data by constructing models which will extract the profound value your data possess. In case you have just stated the question you want to answer, we can give you a valuable advice on designing your (medical) experiment or on a structure of your data to be extracted from your systems. Thereafter the data will be in a convenient form for the subsequent analysis, which will make life lot easier too.

Financial Planning Models

Being the manager and having the responsibility to allocate your assets and/or liabilities to make the most of the opportunity, while controlling for underlying risks is a difficult job. We develop sophisticated stochastic optimization models which offer valuable perspectives for the manager's decision. These are based on advanced mathematical techniques of stochastic programming, when one generates scenarios of future evolution of relevant variables and simulates company's behaviour under such scenarios in order to find the optimal decision. Such models are widely applicable, for example investment decisions, asset-liability management problems or asset allocation on a stock market are typical areas where these models can provide great advantage.
